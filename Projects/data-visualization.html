Ensign College CIS Dashboard - Overview

×

This comprehensive dashboard was developed for Ensign College's Career Internship Services (CIS) department to track and analyze student engagement with career services. The dashboard provides real-time insights into student interactions, mentor activities, and program effectiveness.

Key Achievements

  • 67% Increase in Student Engagement: Helped increase the number of students who attended career internship services from 300 to 500 students
  • Data-Driven Decision Making: Enabled administrators to make informed decisions based on real-time analytics
  • Mentor-Student Interaction Tracking: Comprehensive monitoring of interactions between career internship mentors and students
  • Performance Metrics: Real-time tracking of key performance indicators and service utilization

Ensign College CIS Dashboard - Analytics

×

This detailed analytics view of the CIS Dashboard provides in-depth insights into mentor-student interactions, service utilization patterns, and program effectiveness metrics. The dashboard tracks various KPIs to help optimize career services delivery.

Analytics Features

  • Interaction Tracking: Detailed monitoring of mentor-student communication and engagement patterns
  • Service Utilization: Analysis of which career services are most and least utilized
  • Trend Analysis: Historical data visualization to identify patterns and trends
  • Performance Optimization: Data-driven insights to improve service delivery and student outcomes
